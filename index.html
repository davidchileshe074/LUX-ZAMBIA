Who Are We?

Lux Destinations Travel and Tours is a professional, registered tour operator based in Zambia. We are highly specialized in providing amazing and memorable travel experiences in some of the most attractive African destinations and other parts of the world.

Founded in 2021 by Ms. Tanya Kasanda, we strive to be a travel and tour operator that can easily be depended on when choosing travel destinations and tour packages, especially in Zambia. We professionally handle both local and international services, including Car Rental, Private Charters, Events Planning, and Hotel Accommodation.

Our Vision

To be a well-trusted, reliable travel and tour operator ensuring customer satisfaction through excellent service, whilst contributing to the community and being kind to the environment.

Our Mission

To grow into a market leader by achieving high levels of client satisfaction through excellent service, competitive prices, and honest business ethics while contributing towards sustainable eco-tourism in Zambia. We believe local tourism should be made accessible and favourably priced for all.
